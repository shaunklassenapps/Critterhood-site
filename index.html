Features

Everything you need to stay aware

A simple set of tools for reporting, tracking, and staying informed about wildlife activity nearby.

🐾

Report a sighting

Drop a pin, pick the species and severity, and add a note or photo. It takes less than a minute.

🗺️

Live sightings map

See what's been reported nearby, filter by species or time window, and tap any pin for the full details.

📍

Alert zones

Watch the places that matter most — home, school, or a favourite trail — and get notified when something's spotted close by.

⏰

Alert schedule

Choose the days and hours you want to hear from Critterhood, so alerts land only when you want them.

📖

Wildlife safety guides

Species-specific guidance on what to do if you encounter local wildlife, plus links to official resources like WildSafeBC and Parks Canada.

🔒

Anonymous by design

No account, no sign-in, no personal profile. Sightings you submit are never linked to your identity.

How it works

From sighting to alert in three steps

Someone spots wildlife

A nearby Critterhood user reports what they saw, where, and how close it came.

Critterhood checks your zones

If the sighting falls inside one of your alert zones and your scheduled hours, it's flagged for you.

You get notified

A push notification lets you know — so you can keep an eye out before you head outside.
